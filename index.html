Why Saltend Matters to the Chemical Cluster
Saltend is a CHP plant — it supplies process steam as well as electricity. Units going offline affects:
INEOS Acetyls — acetic acid & acetic anhydride (900,000 t/yr, Europe's largest)
Croda International — oleochemicals & esters, continuous steam requirement
BP / INEOS — ethylene derivatives, ethyl acetate
Nippon Gohsei — EVOH (world's largest single line)
Running status is also a direct proxy for Humber industrial gas demand.
Data: Elexon BMRS API — data.elexon.co.uk/bmrs/api/v1 — free, no key.
PN (live): Physical Notifications — 30 min updates, near real-time generation intent.
B1610 (history): actual reconciled metered output, available from 2015 to ~5 days ago.
REMIT: mandatory outage notices for planned & unplanned trips.
